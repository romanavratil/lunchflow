Our Story

A Passion for
Honest Food

Nestled in the heart of the city, The Garden Bistro was born from a simple belief: the best meals start with the best ingredients. We partner with over a dozen local farms within 50 miles.

Every dish on our menu changes with the seasons. Our chefs work directly with farmers each morning to decide what's worth celebrating today.

No shortcuts. No freezers. Just beautiful, honest food.
